FAQ

How do I sign up? Follow this link to sign up to be added to our email list!
UC Wakeboarding Campus Link
How can I find out about updates? To find out about all the latest news, you can like us on Facebook, follow us on Twitter, and sign up to be added to our email list. Also, you can check back here on our homepage!
Where will we wakeboard? Most of our trips will be to a cable park in Fairfield called Wake Nation. We also want to take a trip to Lake Norris, but as of now, there is no date planned. As always, check our social media to keep up to date!
Do I need my own gear? At this point, we have three helmets, four life jackets, and one wakeboard. Of course, these numbers will grow as the club grows, but you are always welcome to bring your own equipment.
What if I don't know how to wakeboard? No worries! If you don't know how to wakeboard, there is plenty of time to learn! Wake Nation has a training pond for beginners to help you get the hang of it.
